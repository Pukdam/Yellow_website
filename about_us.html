About Yellow

Great food, cocktails and wine

Yellow, is a neighborhood bar and eatery with good eats & great drinks at it’s core.
Our Cocktails are based on high quality spirits and only the freshest ingredients, ranging through the wide spectrum of styles. They are great as a compliment to our food but also stand strong on their own. Our kitchen cooks contemporary bar food and consist of different snacks and sharing friendly dishes. Together with the best natural wine & locally crafted beers we can get our hands on, we can set a chilled vibe or get any party started. On Weekends we turn up the volume and bust a few moves, when some of Copenhagens finest DJ’s drop by to spin records.

Our events

Want to celebrate at Yellow? Get in touch!

We are always ready to host your birthday, summer party, Christmas lunch or any other reason you can come up with to celebrate. Whether it’s food and drinks or only drinks, we can serve up to 100 people inside Yellow.
